Air Fryer Quesadillas

Description

The following is a recipe for easy to make air fryer quesadillas. They are a simple meal with little cleanup but still very tasty. They turn out golden and crispy without having to wash a skillet.

Ingredients

  • two flour tortillas
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheese
  • nonstick cooking spray

Steps

  1. Heat tortillas in microwave until soft, about 15 seconds.
  2. Place 1/4 cup of cheese on one half of each tortilla; fold other half of tortilla over cheese. Place in air fryer. Spray with non-stick cooking spray.
  3. Set temperature to 375 degrees F. Place quesadillas in the basket of the air fryer, and cook until golden brown, 4 to 6 minutes. Flip quesadillas, press with spatula and air fry an additional 2 to 3 minutes.
  4. Serve and enjoy.
